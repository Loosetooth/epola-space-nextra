List of most helpful books

Physics textbooks by

1. S. Borowitz and A. Beiser, **Essentials of Physics**, Addison-Wesley, Mass., 1967.
2. O.H. Blackwood, W.C. Kelly and R.M. Bell, **General Physics**, J. Wiley, N.Y. 1973.
3. C.E. Swartz, **Phenomenal Physics**, J. Wiley, N.Y. 1981.

Solid State Physics books by

4. N.F. Mott and R.W. Guerney, **Electronic Processes in Ionic Crystals**, Oxford University Press, 1946.

6. C. Kittel, **Introduction to Solid State Physics**, J. Wiley, N.Y. 1976.

10. E.U. Condon and H. Odishaw (Ed), **Handbook of Physics**, McGraw Hill, N.Y. 1967.

12. W.C. Michels (Ed), **The International Dictionary of Physics and Electronics**, D. van Nostrand, N.Y. 1957.
